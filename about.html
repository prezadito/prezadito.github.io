About

I am a Social Justice Hacktivist and self-taught programmer with a degree in Political Science. My background in youth mentoring and leadership fuels me to develop solutions for under-seved communities of color. I believe with creativity, collaboration and community-oriented thinking, we can solve much of the problems that plague the world today.

When I'm not hacking into the NSA database, I like to draw, listen to Hip-hop, read comics, play anything competitive, and watch videos that explain the origin of the Universe.
